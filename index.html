⚔️ Battle Simulator Tutorial

🏗️ Step 1: Build Your Base

Left Panel: Click on a building from the "Buildings" tab and click on the canvas to place it.

  • 🏰 Town Hall: Your command center (1000🪙)
  • 🏹 Archer Tower: Shoots enemies from distance (800🪙)
  • 🔫 Cannon: Heavy damage defender (1200🪙)
  • 🧱 Wall: Blocks enemy paths (100🪙)
  • 📦 Storage: Stores resources (600🪙)

Tips: Place towers and cannons to defend. Surround important buildings with walls!

🗡️ Step 2: Train Your Army

Right Panel - Units Tab: Select how many units to train and click "Train"

  • 🗡️ Barbarian: Basic melee unit (50⚡)
  • 🏹 Archer: Ranged attacker (75⚡)
  • 🐉 Dragon: Flying powerhouse (200⚡)
  • 🧙 Wizard: Magic attacks (150⚡)

Note: You spend Elixir (⚡) to train units, not Gold (🪙)

📊 Step 3: Upgrade Your Units

Right Panel - Upgrades Tab: Spend Elixir to boost your units

  • Barbarian Strength: +5 damage per level
  • Archer Precision: +2 range per level
  • Tower Defense: +20% tower damage
  • Wall Fortification: +50 HP per level

Max level: Level 5 for each upgrade

⚔️ Step 4: Attack & Simulate Battle

Bottom Left: Click "⚔️ Test Attack" to simulate a battle

  • Your trained army attacks your own base
  • Your towers and cannons defend
  • Battle Log shows what happens
  • If base is destroyed: WIN! (get 1000🪙 + 500⚡)
  • If base survives: You lose (but learn what to improve)

🎮 Game Controls

  • 🗑️ Clear: Remove all buildings, get 100🪙 back
  • 🎲 Random: Auto-generate a base layout (costs 500🪙)
  • ⚔️ Test Attack: Simulate a battle with your army

💡 Strategy Tips

  • Place Town Hall in the center - it's important!
  • Surround towers with walls to protect them
  • Mix different unit types - barbarians tank, archers deal damage
  • Dragons are expensive but very powerful
  • Test your base often to see where it's weak
  • Upgrade units before training more - quality over quantity

💰 Resources

  • 🪙 Gold: Used to build structures
  • ⚡ Elixir: Used to train units and upgrade
  • ❤️ Base HP: Health of your base (0 = destroyed)
