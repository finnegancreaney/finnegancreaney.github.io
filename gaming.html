Victoria 3

Genre: Grand Strategy

Victoria 3 is an ambitious grand strategy game that puts you in control of a nation from 1836 to 1936. You must navigate complex economics, diplomacy, and politics to build your empire.

What I Love: The depth of the economic system and the realistic portrayal of 19th-century politics. Every decision matters.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Crusader Kings 3

Genre: Medieval Dynasty Simulator

CK3 lets you rule a medieval dynasty from 867 AD onwards. You manage family relationships, claim titles, wage wars, and create your own story through centuries of history.

What I Love: The intricate character system and the emergent storytelling. Every playthrough is unique and unpredictable.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

War of Dots

Genre: Tactical Strategy

War of Dots is a unique tactical strategy game with minimalist art style but deep gameplay mechanics. Control your dots and outmaneuver your opponents in battles.

What I Love: The clean aesthetic and challenging tactical puzzles. It's simple to learn but hard to master.

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
